Our Mission – Life, Balanced

We believe in building balance into morden life – taking the time to rest
and unplug from the daily hustle and to focus on ourselves, our
relationships, and the wonder of nature.

How It Works

STEP 1

Book Your Escape

Choose your Outpost, cabin size, and dates. We'll send you the exact address of your cabin upon booking.

STEP 2

Sit Back

You'll get an email and text with your cabin name and keycode. Our team will be available by email and text should you need anything.

STEP 3

Just Be

Lock away your phone, connect with loved once, and enjoy quite time in nature. You'll have everything you need to truly relax.

GIVING BACK TO NATURE

We've partnered with One Tree Planted – every time you book a Getaway, we'll plant a tree.

The Cabins

Sleeping Soundly in Nature

Each cabin has a comfortable queen bed with fresh linens and pillows. Cabins for four have two mqueen beds, sometimes lofted (so that you can fight over the top bunk). Our beds are always in view of a window, so you can wake up in the trees.
